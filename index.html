What Is Climate Change?


Climate change is the alteration of global climate patterns, particulaly since the mid-20th century, attributed largely to the increased levels of atmospheric carbon dioxide produced by the use of fossil fuels.

Causes of Climate Change

  • Transport

  • Agriculture

  • Energy Industry

  • Environment Changes

Effects of Climate Change

  • Temperature Increase

  • Sea-level Rises

  • Changed Rain Patterns

  • More Extreme Weather
